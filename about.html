Hello! :)​

My name is Olivya Striloff, and I'm currently an Interactive Arts + Technology and Business joint major student at Simon Fraser University. I reside near Vancouver, BC. I consider myself first and foremost an artist, but I love and agree with the philosophy that design is "art with a purpose."

​My hobbies include drawing (of course!), jewelry-making, reading, and general surfing of the internet. I love learning about other cultures, psychology, as well as many other subjects; my Wikipedia-surfing habit has made me a connoisseur of many random, but often delightful, factoids. I have an awesome cat named Mucha ("moo•ka"). Fun fact - his namesake is the artist who inspired this project! I am a huge fan of Pokémon + Harry Potter, as well as numerous other fantasy-based "worlds." I would love to be a concept artist to help bring these worlds to life, in a setting where a strong imagination is valued and encouraged.

To me, professional artistry in the appropriate form is what can set a business apart from its competitors in the best possible way. I hope you enjoy the assortment of works I have compiled here, and that they give you a better idea of who I am as an artist and designer!
